About Us

We are two sophomores at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and over the past two years, we've developed a deep appreciation for everything that makes Carolina special. From the campus culture, to the lasting traditions, there's so much that makes this university feel like home -- and we created this site to highlight some of those experiences, and to show what being a Carolina student is really like. We invite you to explore our 2024-2025 recap, and connect with us if you have any questions, or want to know more about UNC! Thanks for visiting, and Go Heels!
