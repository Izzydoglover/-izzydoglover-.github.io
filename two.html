Basketball Season!

UNC Basketball is one of the best parts of the Carolina experience! You'll never forget your first time rushing Franklin street after beating Duke. We love cheering on Coach Davis and the Heels every chance that we get!

Student Life!

Spring in Chapel Hill is just as special as the fall - maybe even better. The campus comes alive with blooming flowers and sunny days, making it the perfect time to relax on the quad, explore Franklin Street, or grab food from some of the best local spots. There's always something to look forward to.
